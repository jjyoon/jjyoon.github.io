Features

Know the current condition of your heart

Technology

Sensors & Components

  • EKG reader
  • Pulse oximetry monitor
  • Optical heart rate monitor

Heart Monitoring

The Pulseband’s heart monitoring technology is state of the art and gives feedback if there’s any abnormality. It constantly monitors your condition so you never have to worry.

Connectivity

  • WiFi
  • Bluetooth LE
  • Cellular Edge Chip

Motion

Includes accelerometer, gyroscope, and GPS to track the user’s position in space and location in case of emergencies.

Water Resistance

The Pulseband is water resistant up to 10m so feel free to wear it while showering, exercising, swimming and more.

Battery

The Pulseband has a battery that lasts for over a day on a single charge, but we recommend charging it at least once a day (a full charge takes about 1.5 hours).

Syncing

The Pulseband and the mobile app will automatically sync if they’re in close proximity so you’ll have the most updated data every time you check your mobile app.
